*                                                                                  *
*  This README file refers to the data reported in the manuscript "Toxicity and    *
*  utilization of chemical weapons: Does toxicity and venom utilization            *
*  contribute to the formation of species communities?"                            *
*  published in Ecology & Evolution.                                               *
*                                                                                  *
*  Authors: Fabian Ludwig Westermann, Tappey Jones, Lesley Milicich and            *
*           Philip John Lester                                                     *
*                                                                                  *
*  Data was collected by Fabian Ludwig Westermann.                                 *
*                                                                                  *
*  The experimental design is explained in the publication in the methods section  *
*  with the same name as the datafile.                                             *
*                                                                                  *
*  This data is released to the public domain under Creative Commons Zero          *
*  (http://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/).                            *
*                                                                                  *
*                                                                                  *
************************************************************************************
*                                                                                  *
*  1) The table "Arena fight and venom usage.csv" corresponds to the               *
*  manuscript sections of the same name and contains the following columns:        *
*                                                                                  *
*  Date:                                                                           *
*  The date this experiment was conducted                                          *
*                                                                                  *
*                                                                                  *
*  Container:                                                                      *
*  ID of Container in which the experiment was conducted                           *
*                                                                                  *
*  Replicate:                                                                      *
*  Replicate number for this species/density combination                           *
*                                                                                  *
*                                                                                  *
*  Monomorium species:                                                             *
*  The species of Monomorium ants used in this experiment                          *
*                                                                                  *
*                                                                                  *
*  Argentine ant density:                                                          *
*  The number of Argentine ant workers used in this replicate                      *
*                                                                                  *
*                                                                                  *
*  Argentine ant Mortality (divided in 3 subcolumns):                              *
*  Absolute number of dead Argentine ants after 1, 4 and 24 hours.                 *
*                                                                                  *
*                                                                                  *
*  Monomorium Mortality (divided in 3 subcolumns):                                 *
*  Absolute number of dead Monomorium ants after 1, 4 and 24 hours.                *
*                                                                                  *
*                                                                                  *
*  Individual Aggression (divided in 5 subcolumns):                                *
*  Absolute number of observed occurences of aggression (scored from 0 - 4)        *
*  between individual ant workers for each behaviour category within that          *
*  replicate                                                                       *
*                                                                                  *
*                                                                                  *
*  Maximum Aggression (divided in 5 subcolumns):                                   *
*  Absolute number of observed occurences where the respective behaviour category  *
*  (scored from 0 - 4) within that replicate was the highest displayed aggression  *
*  in the entire arena between Monomorium sp. and Argentine ant workers            *
*                                                                                  *
*                                                                                  *
*  Venom usage (divided in 3 subcolumns):                                          *
*  Absolute number of observed occurences of venom usage type (Nn = None,          *
*  Gast = Gaster flagging/spraying, Stng = Stinging)                               *
*                                                                                  *
*                                                                                  *
*  Individual Aggression Rel % (divided in 5 subcolumns):                          *
*  Relative Number of observed occurences of aggression (scored from 0 - 4)        *
*  between individual ant workers for each behaviour category within that          *
*  replicate; calculated from absolute numbers of Individual Aggression            *
*                                                                                  *
*                                                                                  *
*  Maximum Aggression Rel % (divided in 5 subcolumns):                             *
*  Relative number of observed occurences where the respective behaviour category  *
*  (scored from 0 - 4) within that replicate was the highest displayed aggression  *
*  in the entire arena between Monomorium sp. and Argentine ant workers;           *
*  calculated from absolute numbers of Maximum Aggression                          *
*                                                                                  *
*                                                                                  *
*  Venom usage Rel % (divided in 3 subcolumns):                                    *
*  Absolute number of observed occurences of venom usage type (Nn = None,          *
*  Gast = Gaster flagging/spraying, Stng = Stinging)                               *
*                                                                                  *
*                                                                                  *
*  Combined Rel% (divided in 2 subcolumns)                                         *
*  Weighted aggression score (0 - 4) calculated on the sum of relative %           *
*  occurences of the respective behaviour category within that replicate for       *
*  Individual Aggression and Maximum Aggression                                    *
*                                                                                  *
*                                                                                  *
************************************************************************************
*                                                                                  *
*  2) The table "Arena fight and venom usage Argentine ant Survival.csv"           *
*  corresponds to the manuscript section "Arena fight and venom usage". It         *
*  contains data represented in the Argentine ant Mortality column in table        *
*  "Arena fight and venom usage.csv" formatted for a survival analysis. It         *
*  contains the following columns:                                                 *
*                                                                                  *
*                                                                                  *
* Species:                                                                         *
*  The species of Monomorium ants used in this experiment                          *
*                                                                                  *
*                                                                                  *
*  Replicate:                                                                      *
*  Replicate number for this species/density combination, the corresponding        *
*  replicate can be found in "Arena fight and venom usage.csv"                     *
*                                                                                  *
*                                                                                  *
*  SubjectNumber:	                                                           *
*  Individual ant subject within the replicate                                     *
*                                                                                  *
*                                                                                  *
*  Argentine ant density:                                                          *
*  The number of Argentine ant workers used in this replicate                      *
*                                                                                  *
*                                                                                  *
*  Survival Time:	                                                           *
*  The time after which the status (Alive/Dead) was assessed                       *
*                                                                                  *
*                                                                                  *
*  Status:	                                                                   *
*  The status of the Argentine ant worker (0 = Alive; 1 = Dead) of the individual  *
*  subject after the given time.                                                   *
*                                                                                  *
*                                                                                  *
************************************************************************************
*                                                                                  *

*  columns:                                                                        *

*                                                                                  *

*                                                                                  *
*                                                                                  *

*                                                                                  *
*                                                                                  *
*  SubjectNumber:	                                                           *
*  Individual ant subject within the replicate                                     *
*                                                                                  *
*                                                                                  *
*  Argentine ant density:                                                          *
*  The number of Argentine ant workers used in this replicate                      *
*                                                                                  *
*                                                                                  *
*  Survival Time:	                                                           *
*  The time after which the status (Alive/Dead) was assessed                       *
*                                                                                  *
*                                                                                  *

*  The Monomorium venom used in this experiment                                    *
*                                                                                  *
*                                                                                  *

*                                                                                  *
*                                                                                  *

*                                                                                  *
